Our Chai Story

From the bustling streets of India to your cup, we bring you the authentic taste of traditional chai, crafted with passion and served with love.

Born from Tradition

Chai Wala started as a small stall on the streets of Mumbai, where our founder first learned the art of brewing the perfect cup of chai from his grandfather.

After 20 years of perfecting recipes and sourcing the finest ingredients from across India, we decided to share our passion with the world.

Every cup we serve carries the warmth of Indian hospitality and the authenticity of generations of chai-making tradition.

🍃

Fresh Ingredients

We source our tea leaves from the finest estates in Assam and Darjeeling, and our spices are ground fresh daily.

👨‍🍳

Master Craftsmanship

Our chai masters train for years to perfect the art of brewing - each cup is a masterpiece.

❤️

Made with Love

Every cup is prepared with care and served with a smile, following the Indian tradition of hospitality.

🌿

Natural & Pure

No artificial flavors, no preservatives - just pure, natural ingredients that nature gave us.
